Grilled Cheese

Grilled Cheese is the ultmate comfort food and is very customizable. It is a viable college kid meal since there are not a lot of ingredients needed. The directions to make it is so simple and doesnt take more than 20 minutes. It also goes great with differnt soups but the most popular soup to have with a grilled cheese is tomato soup!


Things to add to Grill Cheese

  1. Bacon
  2. More Cheese
  3. Veggies (Grilled Onins, Peppers and Mushrooms)
  4. Tomato, Basil, Mozzarella
  5. Whatever you want, chef it up!
